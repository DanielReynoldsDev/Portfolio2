About Me

I'm a trainee web developer with a keen interest in all things technology! From a young age I've always been drawn to computing and solving puzzles, as well as deconstructing mechanical and electronic devices to find out how they worked.

Throughout my life, aside from being Tech Support for friends and family, I have made small projects in HTML dating back to when CSS was in its infancy, and having to use table elements for styling!, Something I would never do now! I have built some more complex, modern sites for friends, some of which utilize API's and simple JavaScript / macros for online gaming between friends.

I use a PC I built and configured from scratch, a feat I always wanted to accomplish. I have also built several PC systems for friends. I'm hoping to find a career with an organisation that allows me to not just write code, but learn and grow both as a person and as a full stack web developer.
